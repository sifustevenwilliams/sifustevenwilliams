How much do you charge for your lessons?


At the Crosby venue, I charge £8 for one class or £12 for two classes on the same evening (£16 for all three on the Saturday).

At present there is no membership fee but there is a special concession rate of £6 per class.

Private lesson costs might vary depending upon what you want; a standard private lesson would cost £20 per hour.

Workshops and outreach classes would vary considerably depending upon numbers and travel costs.


Drop me a line via the Contact page if you'd like to find out more.
