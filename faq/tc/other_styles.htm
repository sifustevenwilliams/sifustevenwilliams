"Lam Style Tai Chi"? What is that?


There are several styles of Tai Chi, some of the most famous being: Chen; Yang; Wu; Sun; and Ng. Although different from each other, they are all recognisable as being from the same family and share much in common.

Having studied the Yang and Sun styles, Master Lam integrated his understanding of other internal arts into his study of Tai Chi. Tao Yin and Zhan Zhuang have a particularly strong influence on this style and Master Lam's extensive study of each of these arts helped to shape what has become known as Lam Style Tai Chi.


"For ten years I worked at developing a system that would
merge the benefits of everything I had learned"

                                                                       Master Lam Kam Chuen

Lam Style Tai Chi is the result of that work.
